# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-07-06

This application is a strictly personal, single-user project operated by a
private individual. It does not serve or collect data from anyone other
than its operator.

- It reads read-only account information (AIS) via the Enable Banking Open
  Banking API, only from bank accounts the operator has personally linked
  and authorized through PSD2 Strong Customer Authentication.
- No payments are ever initiated (no PIS). No bank credentials are stored
  or automated.
- Data is stored exclusively in the operator's private data workspace, is
  never shared with any third party, and can be deleted by the operator at
  any time.

Data protection inquiries can be made through the contact details
registered with Enable Banking.
